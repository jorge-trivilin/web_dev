About me

Who am i?

Senior data analyst and MLOps engineer with 8+ years of experience in CRM (D2C and B2B). A technology enthusiast with strong analytical skills and a proven ability to explore data to generate valuable insights, hypotheses, and actionable strategies.

What do I do?

I help teams not only collect, compile, and develop performance metrics but also design and implement end-to-end machine learning pipelines. I ensure models are production-ready and aligned with business objectives, particularly in customer lifecycle management.

How do I do?

I start by understanding the business objectives, identifying opportunities where data-driven decisions and machine learning models can have a significant impact. This involves developing and validating hypotheses, building and deploying scalable MLOps pipelines, and continuously monitoring model performance to ensure alignment with business goals.
